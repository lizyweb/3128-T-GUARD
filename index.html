Frequently Asked Questions

  • What pest control services do you offer?

    We offer a wide range of pest control services including General Pest Guard, Rodent Guard, Termite Guard, Mosquito Guard, Spider Guard, Bug Guard, Lizard Guard, Woodborer Guard, Snake Guard, and more. We also provide Integrated Pest Management (IPM) and specialized services for specific pests.

  • How long does the pest control treatment last?

    The effectiveness of our pest control treatments varies based on the type of pest and the specific service provided. Most treatments last for several months, and we offer follow-up visits to ensure continued protection. For termite treatments, we offer warranties.

  • Are your pest control treatments safe for children and pets?

    Yes, we use safe and eco-friendly products that are safe for children and pets. Our technicians follow strict safety protocols and advise you on any necessary precautions after treatment to ensure a safe environment for your family.

  • How do I know if I need pest control services?

    If you notice signs of pests, such as droppings, nests, or visible pests like cockroaches, ants, or rodents, it's a good time to call for pest control. Additionally, if you're seeing damage to your property or belongings, we recommend getting professional pest control treatment as soon as possible.

  • Can I prevent pests from coming back after treatment?

    While pest control treatments effectively reduce pests, maintaining a clean environment and taking preventive measures, such as sealing cracks, fixing leaks, and proper waste management, will help prevent pests from returning. We also offer ongoing maintenance services to ensure long-term protection.

  • How often should I schedule pest control treatments?

    The frequency of treatments depends on the type of pest and your property. For general pest control, we recommend scheduling treatments every 3-6 months. For more persistent pests like termites, rodents, or bedbugs, you may need more frequent visits. Our team can provide a customized schedule based on your needs.

  • What areas do you serve?

    We offer our pest control services across various regions. We primarily serve Coimbatore, Chennai, Tirupur, Erode, Pollachi, Ooty, and other surrounding areas. For a complete list of service areas or to inquire about a specific location, feel free to contact us directly.
